About LegalMY

Democratising access to Malaysian legal intelligence through AI-powered research

Our Story

LegalMY is a product of Calmic Sdn Bhd (SSM: 202201031605), Malaysia's technology innovation arm dedicated to transforming how legal professionals work.

Founded in 2025, we recognised a critical gap in Malaysian legal technology: while international legal AI platforms thrived, Malaysian lawyers lacked access to affordable, locally-focused tools for research and case intelligence. Our mission became clear: democratise access to Malaysian legal intelligence.

Today, LegalMY indexes 100,000+ Malaysian court judgments, profiles 1,200+ judges, and serves 500+ legal professionals from solo practitioners to major law firms, in-house counsel teams, and legal researchers.

Our Mission

To empower Malaysian legal professionals with AI-driven research tools that are affordable, accurate, and deeply rooted in Malaysian law.

Our Vision

To become the trusted legal intelligence platform for Malaysia — the first place Malaysian lawyers turn for case research, judge insights, and AI-powered legal analysis.

The Team

LegalMY is built by a passionate team of software engineers, legal technologists, and Malaysian law experts who understand both the technical and judicial landscapes.

Our Values

  • Accessibility: Legal intelligence should be available to all lawyers, regardless of firm size or budget
  • Accuracy: Every judgment, judge statistic, and AI insight must be reliable and carefully verified
  • Malaysian-First: We prioritise Malaysian law, Malaysian courts, and Malaysian legal context over generic AI
  • Transparency: We're transparent about our data sources, AI limitations, and pricing
  • Privacy: Your case details are confidential. We never store or share client information

Important Disclaimer: LegalMY is a product of Calmic Sdn Bhd. We are not affiliated with the Malaysian Bar Council or the Malaysian Judiciary. LegalMY is an independent legal technology company. Our data comes from publicly available court records and official government sources.
