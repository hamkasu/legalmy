Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. No long-term contracts or cancellation fees.

Do you offer annual discounts?

Yes! Pay annually and save 20% on all plans. Switch from monthly to annual billing in your settings at any time.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit cards (Visa, Mastercard), debit cards, and FPX (Malaysian online banking). All payments are processed securely via Stripe.

Is there a free trial?

Our Free plan is essentially a free trial with limited features. Verified lawyers get a 30-day Professional trial after admin approval.
