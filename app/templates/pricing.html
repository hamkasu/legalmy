Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, absolutely! You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. No long-term contracts, no cancellation fees. Your access continues through the end of your billing period.

Do you offer annual billing discounts?

Yes! When you choose annual billing, you'll save 25% compared to monthly pricing. You can upgrade to annual or switch plans anytime in your account settings.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), debit cards, and FPX (Malaysian online banking). All payments are processed securely through Stripe with bank-level encryption.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes! Our Free plan is available to all users with limited features. For individual lawyers, we offer a 14-day free trial of Research & Judge Analytics. No credit card required to start.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Absolutely! You can change your plan anytime. Upgrades take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any charges based on your remaining billing period.

Do you offer discounts for law firms?

Yes! We offer special pricing and volume discounts for law firms and corporations. Contact our sales team at sales@legalmy.com to discuss a custom plan tailored to your needs.
